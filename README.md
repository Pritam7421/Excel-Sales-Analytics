📊 Sales & Financial Performance Analysis | AtliQ Hardwares

An Excel-based analytics project focused on transforming raw sales data into structured reports to evaluate business performance, track profitability, and support strategic decision-making.

🧩 Business Context

AtliQ Hardwares operates across multiple markets, customers, and distribution channels. However, the absence of structured reporting made it difficult to monitor sales performance and profitability effectively.

As a result, key business questions remained unanswered:

Which customers and markets generate the highest revenue?

How is sales performance evolving over time?

Are sales targets being achieved?

What is the profitability across regions?

🎯 Objective

The primary goal of this project is to design structured Excel reports that provide actionable insights into:

Customer-level sales performance

Market performance vs targets

Profitability analysis through P&L reports

Sales growth trends across time

📦 Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of transactional sales data covering:

Fiscal Years: 2019 – 2021

Multiple customers and geographic markets

Product-level sales records

Due to the dataset size and complexity, preprocessing was required before analysis.

🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

Data transformation was carried out using Excel and Power Query:

Removed duplicate entries

Handled missing values

Standardized formats and naming conventions

Corrected inconsistent data types

Structured raw data into analysis-ready tables

📈 Key Analyses
🔹 Customer Performance Analysis

Evaluated Net Sales by customer

Calculated Year-over-Year (YoY) growth

Identified top-performing customers

🔹 Market Performance vs Target

Compared actual sales against targets

Analyzed performance across regions and countries

Highlighted underperforming markets

🔹 Profit & Loss (P&L) Analysis

Generated dynamic P&L reports across:
